*Protocol: Last Light* is set on a hostile planet that was once colonized but now stands abandoned. The story is told through environmental cues and high-tension gameplay rather than traditional dialogue.

The game concludes with a cinematic Transmission Sequence rather than a boss fight:
1. **The Signal:** Once the player reaches 100% Signal, the final sequence triggers.
2. **Super-Fuel:** The player must craft a high-energy cell to power the final transmission.
3. **The Whiteout:** The lighthouse beam turns a blinding white, momentarily clearing the entire planet of the "Darkness."
4. **The Response:** The terminal displays a single incoming text response: *"Signal Received. We see you. We are coming."*
